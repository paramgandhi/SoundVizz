Year

This interactive allows you to explore the history of music. Follow the instructions to navigate this interactive.

Instructions:

Please use the slider below to choose a year and view the characteristics of music released through that year on the radar plot on the left.

Year: 2010

Key insights:

  • Music before the 1950's contained a lot of acoustic elements.
  • Post 1950, the music started to include more danceability and tempo.
  • After 1970, the music included higher values of loudness, energy and tempo.
